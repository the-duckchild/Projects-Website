Rubbing Out Project - PQ Festival Prague, 2023

I collaborated with artists Andrea Carr and Gemma Riggs, and produced a generative soundscape to accompany the artwork Rubbing Out Project for the 2023 Prague Quadrennial Festival of Performance Design and Space. at the Holešovice Market, Prague, Czechia.

In Our Own Time

I composed music to accompany this project by visual artist Gemma Riggs, as part of the Creative Europe project "Magic Carpets". The work was displayed at Superorganism, Zagreb, Croatia (May 2019) Festival of Looking, Folkestone, UK (July 2019) Magic Carpets Landed / Kaunas Biennial Kaunas, Lithuania (Nov 2021 – Jan 2023)
